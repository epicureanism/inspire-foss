This viewer renders Dutch INSPIRE data as WMS layers. The current themes supported are Cadastral Parcels (CP) and Addresses (AD). Data is present for selected parts of The Netherlands : for CP the area of South Limburg (near Maastricht) and for AD those towns that had address-data from the basic adminstration (BAG) available. Currently there are no areas that have both CP and AD available.


To view AD, please click here (zooms to Tilburg).


To view CP, please click here (zooms to South Limburg).


The projection of this map is ETRS89 (EPSG:4258) as required for INSPIRE. This projection is similar to WGS84 (EPSG:4326) hence The Netherlands looks "broader" than in the usual projections.
